# 📊 Customer Retention & Churn Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Objective

The objective of this project is to analyze customer behavior and identify key factors contributing to churn in a subscription-based business. The goal is to uncover actionable insights that help improve customer retention and reduce churn rate.

---

## 🛠️ Tools Used

* Power BI
* Data Cleaning & Transformation
* DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
* Data Visualization & Dashboarding

---

## 📁 Dataset

This project uses a customer subscription dataset containing:

* Customer demographics (gender, senior citizen, partner, dependents)
* Account details (tenure, contract type, payment method)
* Services (internet service, phone service, security features)
* Billing information (monthly charges, total charges)
* Churn status (Yes/No)

---

## 📊 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

* Total Customers: **7,000+**
* Churned Customers: **2,000+**
* Churn Rate: **27%**
* Average Customer Tenure: **32.42 months**

---

## 📈 Dashboard Features

* Churn Analysis by Contract Type
* Churn Analysis by Internet Service
* Churn Analysis by Payment Method
* Churn vs Tenure Trend
* Interactive Filters (Slicers)
* KPI Cards for quick business overview

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

* **High churn rate (~27%)** indicates that nearly 1 in 4 customers leave the service.
* Customers on **month-to-month contracts** have the highest churn compared to long-term contracts.
* **Fiber optic users** contribute the largest share of churn, suggesting possible service or pricing issues.
* Customers using **electronic check payment methods** churn more than those using automated payments.
* Churn is highest in the **early months (0–6 months)**, indicating onboarding and initial experience issues.
* Long-term customers (higher tenure) show significantly better retention and loyalty.

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

* Promote long-term contracts (annual plans) with discounts and incentives.
* Improve service quality and pricing strategies for fiber optic customers.
* Encourage customers to switch to **AutoPay** methods through offers or benefits.
* Strengthen onboarding experience and engagement during the first 90 days.
* Implement targeted retention strategies for high-risk customer segments.

---

## 🎯 Priority Actions

| Priority | Action                            | Impact    |
| -------- | --------------------------------- | --------- |
| High     | Reduce month-to-month churn       | Very High |
| High     | Improve fiber customer retention  | Very High |
| High     | Increase AutoPay adoption         | High      |
| Medium   | Improve early customer experience | High      |
| Medium   | Promote long-term contracts       | Medium    |

---

## 📌 Conclusion

The analysis reveals that churn is primarily driven by short-term contracts, high-cost service users, manual payment methods, and early-stage customer drop-off. By addressing these factors, the business can significantly improve retention and reduce churn below 20%.
